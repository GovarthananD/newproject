Kazi Nazrul Islam

The national poet of Bangaladesh

Rebel Poet

Kazi Nazrul Islam is the national poet of Bangaladesh. who is especially recognized as a Bidrohi Kobi(rebel poet) arount the country. Nazrul is a famous poet, musician and philospher of Bangaladesh. The famous poet Nazrul's writing deals with the revloution of the period of the British Government. His revloutionary attempt helped him to be a national poet of Bangaladesh. He has raised his voice against any kind of facism and opperassion and got popularity swifitly around the indian subcontinent.




Awsome Writer

Nazrul's writing explored themes such as love, freedom, humanity and evloution. He opposed all form of bigtory and fundamentalism including religious, cast based and gender based. Through out is career, Nazrul's wrote short stories, novels and essay bus is best know for his song and poems. He poineered new music form such as bengali ghazls.Nazrul's worte and composed music for nearly 4000 songs.
